Why Looper Exists

Two goals that change your game

1

Know exactly what club to hit — every time

Looper builds a personalised yardage profile for every club in your bag from your own launch monitor data. On the course, tell it the distance, wind, elevation, lie, or conditions — and it gives you a confident recommendation based on how you actually hit the ball, not a generic chart.

2

Improve through coaching built on your own data

Looper analyses your shot patterns to identify what's genuinely holding you back — then points you to specific drills and videos to fix it. The result: real improvements in accuracy, consistency, and distance, grounded in your game rather than generic advice.

What Looper Does

Everything your game needs

Looper connects to your launch monitor data and gives you the kind of analysis that used to require a professional fitting or coaching session.

📊

Launch Monitor Analysis

Import your GSPro, Garmin golf or other session data and get instant breakdowns of ball speed, spin rate, carry distance, and more — per club.

🏌️

Swing Fault Detection

Looper identifies patterns across your shots and flags recurring faults with recommended drills to fix them.

🎯

Club Recommendations

Tell Looper your distance, wind, lie and hazards — it calculates the optimal club using your actual distance data.

📈

Progress Tracking

Track trends across sessions and see how your ball striking, consistency, and distances evolve over time.

🎓

Coaching Library

Browse drills, videos, and coaching topics curated to your specific swing faults and skill level.

🎯

GPS Rangefinder

Real-time GPS distance to the green from anywhere on the course. Auto-selects the correct hole, shows front/back/centre distances, and highlights landing zones and doglegs ahead of you.

🗺️

Golf Round Planner

Set a score goal and get a personalised hole-by-hole strategy built from your actual carry distances. Recalibrates automatically as you play.

👨‍🏫

Coach in the Loop

Human coaches can connect to their students through Looper to review data, send drills, and monitor progress remotely.

The Company

About Parity Golf

Parity Golf is a Swedish technology company building intelligent tools that make high-quality golf coaching accessible to every player — not just those with access to expensive instructors or fitting studios.

We believe the gap between amateur and tour-level play isn't just physical — it's informational. Launch monitors and AI now make it possible to close that gap. Looper is our first step.

Founded and based in Bromma, Stockholm, Sweden.

Our mission is to give every golfer the same quality of feedback and coaching insight that professionals take for granted — delivered through the data their own game produces.

Looper is available on iOS and is designed for golfers who use launch monitors like Garmin R10 / R50 (and others) to practice and improve.
