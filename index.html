What Looper Does

Everything your game needs

Looper connects to your launch monitor data and gives you the kind of analysis that used to require a professional fitting or coaching session.

📊

Launch Monitor Analysis

Import your GSPro, Garmin golf or other session data and get instant breakdowns of ball speed, spin rate, carry distance, and more — per club.

🏌️

Swing Fault Detection

Looper identifies patterns across your shots and flags recurring faults with recommended drills to fix them.

🎯

Club Recommendations

Tell Looper your distance, wind, lie and hazards — it calculates the optimal club using your actual distance data.

📈

Progress Tracking

Track trends across sessions and see how your ball striking, consistency, and distances evolve over time.

🎓

Coaching Library

Browse drills, videos, and coaching topics curated to your specific swing faults and skill level.

👨‍🏫

Coach in the Loop

Human coaches can connect to their students through Looper to review data, send drills, and monitor progress remotely.

Three AI Personas

The right voice for every situation

Looper adapts its coaching style depending on what you need — whether you're on the range, on the course, or diving deep into your numbers.

Coach

The Coach

Identifies your faults, builds a practice plan, and guides you through structured improvement with drills and progressions.

Caddie

The Caddie

On-course strategy and club selection. Tell it your distance, wind and hazards — it recommends the smart play based on your data.

Analyst

The Analyst

Deep dive into your metrics. Compare to tour benchmarks, identify outlier shots, and understand what the numbers actually mean.

For Golf Professionals

Connect with your students through Looper

Looper isn't just for solo golfers. Real coaches can link directly to their students' accounts to monitor progress, assign drills, and deliver feedback — remotely or in person.

🔗

Connect to Students

Students invite you to their Looper account with a simple code. Once connected, you have a live view of their practice data and session history.

📋

Review Session Data

See exactly what your student hit on the range — ball speed, spin, carry, dispersion — without needing to be there in person.

🏋️

Assign Drills

Push specific drills and practice tasks directly to your student's app. They show up in their coaching queue ready to follow.

📬

Send Feedback

Leave notes and observations on sessions. Students get your feedback in context — tied to the specific shots or trends you're commenting on.

📈

Track Progress Over Time

See how your students improve across weeks and months. Trend charts show you whether your coaching is moving the needle.

👥

Manage Multiple Students

Switch between students from a single coach dashboard. Each student's data is kept separate and private.

The Company

About Parity Golf

Parity Golf is a Swedish technology company building intelligent tools that make high-quality golf coaching accessible to every player — not just those with access to expensive instructors or fitting studios.

We believe the gap between amateur and tour-level play isn't just physical — it's informational. Launch monitors and AI now make it possible to close that gap. Looper is our first step.

Founded and based in Bromma, Stockholm, Sweden.

Our mission is to give every golfer the same quality of feedback and coaching insight that professionals take for granted — delivered through the data their own game produces.

Looper is available on iOS and is designed for golfers who use launch monitors like Garmin R10 / R50 (and others) to practice and improve.
