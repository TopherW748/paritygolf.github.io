Product Roadmap

Now · Next · Later

This is a living roadmap. Priorities shift as we learn from players and coaches — but these are our current intentions.

🚀

Now

Shipping & in market

Launch

iOS App — Initial Release

Looper is live on the App Store. Connect your launch monitor data, meet your AI Coach, Caddie, and Analyst.

Feature

Personalised Club Profiles

Carry distances, spin rates, and dispersion patterns built from your own GSPro and Garmin session data.

Feature

On-Course Caddie

Ask Looper for a club recommendation and get a data-driven answer accounting for distance, wind, elevation, lie, and hazards.

Feature

Swing Fault Detection & Drills

Pattern analysis across sessions surfaces recurring faults and connects them to targeted drills and videos.

Feature

Coach in the Loop

Human coaches can connect to their students, review session data, assign drills, and track progress remotely.

Feature

Progress Tracking & Benchmarks

Longitudinal trend charts and comparisons against handicap-level and PGA Tour benchmarks.

🔨

Next

In development

Platform

MCP Server Launch

A Model Context Protocol server that lets developers and power users connect Looper's golf intelligence directly to their own AI workflows and tools.

AI

Model Upgrade

Upgrading the underlying language model for sharper coaching responses, better context handling, and faster answers.

Later

On the horizon

Vision

Video Swing Analysis

Record your swing and get AI-powered frame-by-frame feedback — no expensive launch monitor or instructor session required.

Vision

AI Caddie — Talking Head

A conversational, voice-first caddie experience with a generated avatar you can talk to on the course, hands-free.
